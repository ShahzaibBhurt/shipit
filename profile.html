American Export Lines (AEL) is a full-service international freight forwarding and logistics company founded in 1974 specializing in import, export and foreign to foreign global freight and logistics services including: Ocean, Air, Land and Rail Freight, warehousing, NVOCC, project and heavy lift cargo, and customs brokerage services. AEL also offers logistics services into and out of “hard to reach” and/or “hard to ship”, including destinations with complex import and/or export licensing requirements. With a network of reliable agents, an experienced staff and our own warehouses in the East and West coast, and shipment visibility, AEL offers full-service customized freight solutions to clients worldwide.

Customer service is our first priority, led by our competitive rates and operational excellency. Being a professional freight forwarder is not enough at AEL. We believe in our clients as partners; their success is our success. Constant attention to expediting your shipment is just part of the routine at our offices. We are proud of the loyalty of our employees most of whom have been with the company for over 15 years.

Mission:
To deliver an exceptional customer experience through respect, integrity, reliability, professionalism, attention to detail, and competitive pricing using effective communication, having a passion for our work, and a dedication to our partners and customers worldwide.

Vision:
To provide the best customer experience, competitive pricing and be the “Top Choice” to our partners and customers worldwide for international freight forwarding and logistics services.
